Staff Applications.

This is the section for staff apps. Thanks for consuming time to apply for our server.


FAQ (read before applying):

 What do i need to apply for staff?

To apply for staff on SimpleCraft you need atleast 3 days of playtime, and decent reputation on the server. Being a supporter thru voting casually or donating is not required, but it might help in the final verdict.

When will i receive the verdict of my application?

The verdict of your application will be delivered at the email and the discord account you provided, at the time that Staff applications will be over.

Can i apply for Admin or Mod or rank X?

No you may not apply for a specific staff rank. Every accepted staff member will start from Helper rank and will work their way up.

What are the staff ranks?

Staff ranks from lowest to highest are:
Helper > Moderator > Admin > SuperAdmin > HeadAdmin

Is there a specific format i have to follow?

Yes, you must follow this application Format:

Minecraft IGN(in game username) : ex.BladesMasterX
Age : ex.18
Discord ID: ex. BladesMaster #6408 (discord is required)
Do you own a donor rank on the server? (yes or no) and the rank (if you have one)
Do you vote frequently for the server (10+ votes)? (yes or no)
Do you have any past staff experience on other servers? (max 100 words)
Why should we make you staff on our server? (max 100 words)
