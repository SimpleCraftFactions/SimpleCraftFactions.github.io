Current event -  win supporter rank!

In this event, you are able to win for free, a supporter rank on our server which includes:
-/tptoggle (prevent people from tping to you)
-/craft (portable crafting table)
-/echest (portable ender chest)
-/kit supporter (full iron II set + 16 ender pearls + food)
-/top (teleport to the top surface)

In order to earn this rank, all you have to do is invite your friends to the server!
In detail, to do that you have to do the following steps:
1)Invite your friend to the server
2) Enter command /refer (friends name)
3)Your friend has to click on your invite to accept it or type /referaccept (your refer code)


The player that invites the most people in the server by the end of this year, wins supporter rank.
